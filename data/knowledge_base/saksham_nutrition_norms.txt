SAKSHAM / POSHAN 2.0 / ICDS NUTRITION NORMS FOR PREGNANT WOMEN
================================================================

POSHAN 2.0 (PM Poshan Shakti Nirman):
- Integrated nutrition support program for pregnant and lactating women
- Works through Anganwadi centres (AWC) at village level
- Provides supplementary nutrition, health education, and growth monitoring

DAILY NUTRITIONAL REQUIREMENTS FOR PREGNANT WOMEN:

First Trimester (Weeks 1-12):
- Calories: 1800 kcal/day (no additional calories needed over non-pregnant)
- Protein: 55 g/day
- Iron: 30 mg/day
- Folic acid: 400 mcg/day (5mg if previous NTD)
- Calcium: 600 mg/day
- Key focus: Folic acid for neural tube development

Second Trimester (Weeks 13-27):
- Calories: 2140 kcal/day (+340 kcal over baseline)
- Protein: 70 g/day
- Iron: 60 mg/day
- Folic acid: 500 mcg/day
- Calcium: 1000 mg/day
- Key focus: Iron and calcium for fetal growth

Third Trimester (Weeks 28-40):
- Calories: 2250 kcal/day (+450 kcal over baseline)
- Protein: 70 g/day
- Iron: 60 mg/day
- Folic acid: 500 mcg/day
- Calcium: 1000 mg/day
- DHA: 200 mg/day (for brain development)
- Key focus: Sustained nutrition for rapid fetal growth

SUPPLEMENTARY NUTRITION (Through Anganwadi):

Take Home Ration (THR) for Pregnant Women:
- 600 calories and 18-20g protein per day through THR
- Components: Fortified wheat/rice flour, dal, oil, sugar/jaggery
- Special THR for severely malnourished (additional 800 kcal)

Hot Cooked Meal (HCM) at AWC:
- Regular attendees get one hot meal at Anganwadi
- Includes: Rice/roti, dal, vegetable, salad

ANGANWADI-AVAILABLE ITEMS FOR RATION DISTRIBUTION:
1. Rice: 200g/day (carbohydrates, energy)
2. Wheat flour (atta): 150g/day (carbohydrates, protein, fiber)
3. Dal (lentils - toor/moong/masoor): 100g/day (protein, iron)
4. Eggs: 1-2 per day (protein, B12, choline)
5. Milk: 500ml/day (calcium, protein)
6. Groundnuts: 30-50g/day (protein, healthy fats, folate)
7. Jaggery (gur): 20-30g/day (iron, energy)
8. Green leafy vegetables: 150g/day (iron, folic acid, calcium)
9. Seasonal fruits: 100g/day (vitamins, fiber)
10. Soy chunks: 30-50g/day (protein, iron)
11. Fortified flour (atta): 100g/day (iron, folic acid, B12)
12. Oil (mustard/groundnut): 25-30ml/day (essential fatty acids)

SUPPLEMENTS PROVIDED:
1. IFA tablets: 100mg elemental iron + 500mcg folic acid (1 daily)
2. Calcium tablets: 500mg (2 daily from 14 weeks)

CONDITION-SPECIFIC DIETARY RECOMMENDATIONS:

For Anemia (Hb < 11 g/dL):
- Double IFA tablets (2 per day)
- Iron-rich foods: spinach, amaranth, fenugreek, drumstick leaves
- Jaggery with groundnuts daily
- Dates and raisins
- Beetroot and pomegranate
- Vitamin C foods (amla, lime) to enhance iron absorption
- Avoid tea/coffee with meals (inhibits iron absorption)

For Gestational Diabetes (GDM):
- Low glycemic index (GI) diet
- Replace white rice with brown rice/millets (ragi, jowar, bajra)
- Increase dal and vegetable portions
- Reduce sugar, sweets, juices
- Small frequent meals (6 meals/day)
- Include bitter gourd, fenugreek seeds
- Fiber-rich foods: whole grains, vegetables, fruits with skin

For Underweight (BMI < 18.5):
- Extra THR from Anganwadi
- Additional 200-300 kcal/day beyond standard
- Groundnuts and jaggery ladoo
- Banana milkshake
- Extra egg/milk servings
- Energy-dense foods: dry fruits, ghee, sesame seeds

For Overweight (BMI > 25):
- Reduce refined carbohydrates (maida, white rice)
- Increase vegetables and salads
- Control oil/ghee usage
- Maintain protein intake (dal, eggs, milk)
- Regular gentle walking (30 min/day)
- Avoid fried foods and sweets

For Pre-eclampsia/Hypertension:
- Low salt diet (< 5g/day)
- High potassium foods: banana, coconut water, sweet potato
- Calcium-rich foods
- Avoid pickles, papad, processed foods
- Adequate hydration

MONITORING THROUGH ANGANWADI:
- Monthly weight monitoring
- Growth tracking
- Nutrition counseling
- THR distribution and tracking
- Referral to PHC for medical issues
